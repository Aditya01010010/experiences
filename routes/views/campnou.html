Camp Nou

Built in 1957, the Camp Nou is one of the most beautiful stadiums in the whole world. For FC Barcelona fans, it truly is heaven on earth. Capable of housing about a hundred thousand spectators, this stadium has an aura of its own. Amongst the so many prestigious football stadiums across Europe, the Camp Nou holds a special place, filled with magic and wonder.
